How technology can better Construction



how tech is used in Construction and how it made it easier, safer and faster.

tech has been used in many different ways in construstion it's been used to make things faster and less work for the construction workers. We are going to be looking at some apps that you might not know about.

TSheets- it’s an app that allows contractors to track their worker’s schedules from the app. The workers check in on the app and they can put real photos to the app about their progress, what equipment they used on site.

Upkeep- it is an app that makes it where you can schedule repairs quickly. You take a picture of your broken equipment then you can schedule a repair. You can also manage your equipment.
Sage- It allows you to share information with your whole project team anytime and anywhere.
